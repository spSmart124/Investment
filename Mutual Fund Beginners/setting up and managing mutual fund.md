# Setting up and Managing Mutual Fund
## Asset Management Company (AMC)
**AMC** handles day to day operation of mutual fund. The sponsor or trustee if so through a trust deed shall appoint AMC upon approval from SEBI

## Role of AMC
The AMC manages investor's money on a day to day basis. All activities are done in the name of the Trust, that is, the mutual fund.

The AMC floats and manages scheme by buying and selling securities in the name of the Trust.

When a new scheme is to be launched, the AMC submits a Draft Offer Document to SEBI.

After the SEBI approves it, the draft offer document becomes offer document (OD) of the scheme.

The compliance officer has to sign the due dilligence certificate in the OD. The certificate ensures that the AMC takes responsibility of the OD and its contents.

The AMC also appoints intermediaries like independent financial advisors (IFAs), national and regional distributors and banks etc.

It is responsible for the acts of its employees and service providers.

It can not deal with a single broker beyond a certain limit of transactions.

The AMC can not act as Trustee of some other mutual funds.

It charges some fee for its service. It is borne by the investors as a percentage of total investment. However, it can not charge beyond a specified limit.

## SEBI Regulations of AMC
* Directors of AMC should have adequate experience in finance sector
* The directors as well as key personnel of the AMC should not have been convicted of any economic offence
* Key personnel of the AMC should not have worked for any asset management or any intermediary during the period when its registration was suspended or cancelled at any time by SEBI
* Prior approval of Trustees is required, before a person is appointed as director on
