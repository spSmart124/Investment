# Various Market Segments and Products
The exchane **NSE** provides trading in 4 different segments.
* Wholesale Debt Market
* Capital Market
* Futures and Options Segment
* Currency Derivatives Segments

## Wholesale Debt Market
It includes.
* State and Central Government securities
* T-Bills
* PSU Bonds
* Corporate debentures
* Commercial Papers
* Certificate of Deposits etc.

## Capital Market
It provides a screen based trading system using **National Exchange for Automated Trading (NEAT)** system.
It trades equity shares, warrants, debentures etc.

## Futures and Options Segment
It provides trading in derivatives instruments like: 
* Index futures
* Index options
* Stock options
* Stock futures

## Currency Derivatives Segments
It provides current futures trading in
* USD-INR
* Euro-INR
* Pound Sterling-INR
* Japanese Yen-INR
* Interest rate futures
